Digital Identity Audit

A systematic framework for discovering, assessing, and controlling your digital footprint across the internet.

1 Search Engine Footprint

Discover what the world sees when they search for you. We check Google for your name, email, phone, and known usernames across the first 3 pages of results.

Searches performed: Full name exact match, name + role, email address, usernames, phone number

2 Data Breach Exposure

Check Have I Been Pwned and breach databases for your email addresses. Every breach is categorized by severity.

Critical
Passwords + Financial
High
Passwords exposed
Medium
Email + personal data
Low
Email only

3 Social Media Audit

Platform-by-platform review of your profiles: LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Reddit. We assess visibility settings, exposed PII, and professional risk.

4 Data Broker Scan

Check presence on people-search engines: Spokeo, WhitePages, BeenVerified, Pipl, ThatsThem, PeekYou, Radaris. These aggregate public records and sell your data.

5 Account Inventory

Map all known accounts and categorize: Active/Needed, Active/Unused (delete), Cannot Delete (GDPR request), Abandoned (recover then delete). Uses JustDeleteMe difficulty ratings.

6 KVKK/GDPR Templates

Pre-filled data deletion request templates for Turkish KVKK (Law 6698) and EU GDPR (Article 17). Ready to send to any data controller.

Subject: Request for Erasure (GDPR Art. 17) Dear Data Protection Officer, Under Article 17 of the GDPR, I request erasure of all personal data you hold about me. Name: [YOUR NAME] Email: [YOUR EMAIL] Please confirm within 30 days. Regards, [YOUR NAME]

7 Visibility Strategy

Recommendations for controlling your narrative: SEO optimization, content strategy, profile consolidation, privacy hardening, and monitoring via Google Alerts.

8 Audit Report

Final deliverable includes: risk score (1-10), all findings documented, prioritized remediation checklist, pre-filled templates, and monitoring setup instructions.

  • Search engine results documented
  • Breach exposure assessed
  • Social profiles audited
  • Data broker presence mapped
  • Old accounts inventoried
  • Deletion requests prepared
  • Monitoring configured
